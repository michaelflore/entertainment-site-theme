No Game No Life Season 2 Release Date, New Information, Updates 2019

By: The Editors at DevGoblin

May 21, 2019

Introduction

The release date for the series No Game No Life Season 2 is highly anticipated, especially now that anime studio Madhouse is starting to make a name for creating anime sequels. The No Game No Life: Zero movie provided a prequel story that just fed fans appetites for watching more of Shiro and Soras main adventure. Madhouse unfortunately has not said a single world so far about a No Game No Life sequal. It turns out the way the story was published almost required a movie to be made before returning back to the main story.

No Game No Life Plot

A mysterious god welcomes the main characters Sora and Shiro to the disboard, which is a world where all forms of conflict ranging from fighting to war are settled by the way of board games. The fundamental rule is that each party member must wager something they deem to be of equal value to the other partys wager. In this strange land where the very idea of humanity is reduced to a childrens board game, the indifferent genius gamer duo of Sora and Shiro have finally found a real reason to keep playing games. Their purpose is to unite the sixteen races of the disboard, defeat Tet, and become the gods of this newly gaming world.

No Game No Life Season 2 Release Date

As of the latest update, studio Madhouse or any company related to them has not officially confirmed the No Game No Life Season 2 release date. Neither has there been a production regarding a sequel announced. Once the news is officially confirmed this article will be updated with all the relevant information. In the meantime, it is possible to speculate about when, the No Game No Life Season 2 premiere date will occur in the future. The good news is that there is plenty of source material for studio Madhouse to create Season 2. Since one book was turned into the movie, six volumes are available for the second season, leaving room for the possibility of a 24 episode second season. Even if a longer second season does not happen, it would make sense for Madhouse to jump straight into No Game No Life Season 3.

No Game No Life Season 2 Release Date Speculation

The real question is will there ever be another season? First, the second season of No Game No Life has to come out, considering the first season had such a huge sucess generating such a large audience. The series is top rated, and although Madhouse has struggled to produce sequels in the past, they started doing so now at an alarming rate. No Game No Life light novels now have enough content to be adapted into another TV season as well. We should definitely see the show return at some point. While we have not received any information on No Game No Life Season 2 in years, the movie was finsihed just recently. The anime will likely return probably a year or two after the film. A possible predicted release date is No Game No Life will return in Fall 2019.
