Danmachi Season 2 Release date, Confirmation, News 2019

By: The Editors at DevGoblin

May 21, 2019

Introduction

The Danmachi series is a Japanese light novel written and produced by Fujino Omori. It has been illustrated by Suzuhito Yasuda and aired back in April 4, 2015. It is a highly anticipated mainstream anime that recieved alot of hype and attention when it first originally aired. The second season along with a new movie has been confirmed by the studio.

Danmachi Plot

It starts out with a war game kicking off the official plot. Bell is a guy who goes on a mission fighting various cave monsters until one day he stumbles upon a mysterious girl. Hestia is a princess who lives in a dungeon for some specific reason. Belle works in the dungeon of the city Orario. Bell then falls in love with Hestia inside the dungeon when she saves his life from a monster. He is unaware of the fact that many other girls would endear for him just like Hestia.

Danmachi Season 2 release date

There is no official release date for Danmachi season 2, not just yet. First the studio is concentrating on an anime movie extension in order to return to the main plot. The most likely release date for the movie is in the summer of 2019. If this certainly is true, then the season 2 of Danmachi should kick off either sometime in the summer of 2019 or in the beginning of 2020. Fans will have to wait and find out to see what the studio says in the upcoming months.

The anime did very well in streaming services and in revenue. The first season kicked off in 2015 and even has a spin off side story. The light novels are still being made till this day, thus providing an endless amount of content for the fans. The season 2 was confirmed in the event called bunko stage 2018. Ever since then fans have been eager and excited for the new season.

Danmachi Season 2 release date updates 2019

As of the latest update, right now the company is focusing on the movie first and then eventually will focus their attention on the season 2. So far it has been officially confirmed bu still no release date. Stay tuned for any updates we may have on the subject. Once it is confirmed, this article will have all the relevant information it needs.
