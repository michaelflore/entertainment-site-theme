Trinity Seven Season 2 Release Date, Characters, Plot, Episode List 2019

By: The Editors at DevGoblin

May 22, 2019

Plot

The entire plot of Trinity Seven spins around a normal high school boy, named Arata Kasuga. He found out that the world he knows is an illusion and a sudden breakdown phenomenon destroyed the real world in which he used to live in. His cousin Hijiri Kasuga and his friends drove to another dimension when the real world was destroyed. However, everything changes on the day of the black sun, which caused the breakdown phenomenon that ends up destroying the town where he lived. This also separates Aratas childhood friend and cousin into separate dimensions. Later on he decided to finally go look for them. In order to complete his mission, he gets admitted to Royal Biblia Academy where he meets seven people are known as Trinity Seven. Arata Kasuga now has a mission to save his old world and bring his sister back.

Trinity Seven Season 2 Release Date

Well, the first season had huge success but the confirmation of the Trinity Seven Season 2 is still a big question. According to some news sources and rumors the release date of Trinity Seven season 2, following the premiere of the new series will be held in April 11, 2019. This release date is just a speculation and the next fall or even the beginning of 2020 is the most likely th predicted release date for the new season. In the meantime, we are waiting for the official news confirmation. Once the announcement is made this article will be updates as soon as possbile. Until then stay tuned for updates.

Trinity Seven Characters

Arata Kasuga: He is the demon lord of the Superbia archive and the main protagonist of the series. His thema is Impel, which means control. After meeting Lilith Asami he enrolls into Royal Biblia Academy and aspires to become a great mage in order to bring his sister Hijiri back. His personality is calm and he always speaks in a perverted way with others. Other people refer to him as the Demon Lord Candidate.

Hijiri Kasuga: She is disguised as Aratas sister and disappears in the collapse phenomenon which was created by Arata. She is later revived as Iscariot to destroy Trinity Seven and the entire world. She is refered to as the Fallen Saint. Although Arata becomes her enemy, she still has a sisterly love for him. Her thema is Runia, which means destruction and her magus is Ira, which means anger.

Lilith Asami: She is the main girl who always carries a pistol with her and she is the magus of lust. She is the first member of Trinity Seven to befriend Arata Kasuga and her thema is Abie which means life.

Arin Kannazuki: Briefly she is the partner of Demon Lord. She later becomes the closest friend of Arata Kasuga and also claims to be his wife. Her thema is Runia and her magus is anger as her appearance is quite similar to Hijiri Kasuga.

Trinity Seven Season 2 Updates

As always there are rumors going around that the anime is going to be premiered on April 11, 2019, however, this is not an official announcement. The main reason why there is no Season 2 is that there are license problems. Crunchyroll first obtained the license, but in 2015 Madman Entertainment bought obtained the rights for the anime. After that, another company named Sentai Filmworks took over in 2016 in order to continue the unfinished project. The current owner of the anime is now KSM Anime, starting from the year of 2017. Fans are still hoping that there is going to be a Trinity Seven Season 2 in the near future. So far there is a second film adaptation from the current manga, which might be a way to test if there are enough fans of the show. If the movie gets a positive rating, we can ceratinly predict a new season of Trinity Seven.
