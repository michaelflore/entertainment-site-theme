Anime News!

If you are not familiar already, Anime is described as a term for a style of Japanese comic book and cartoon animation including all forms of animated media, in which the main characters have large cartoon like eyes. Typical anime themes or genres can include fighting, martial arts, supernatural events, horror stories, romance, or even science fiction including magic and sorcery. Here in the anime tab you will find everything about anime all in one place. This can be ranging from news articles, videos, or types of images. History of Anime:

Anime had originally been limited to in Japan only starting in the early 1960s. Eventually when the 1990s came around, Japanese directors decided to eventually bring their creations to live television. Once the subject gave rise and had become more popular it eventually expanded to different countries surrounding Japan, eventually heading into western countries such as Mexico, Unites States, and Canada. Today as we know it, more and more anime shows are constantly being produced and translated into various languages for streaming and watching purposes. Anime today continues to spread throughout the world and is becoming a mainstream topic for television.
